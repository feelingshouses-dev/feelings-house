# Email Setup Guide - Feelings Houses

## Email Addresses Needed
1. **info@feelingshouses.gr** - Γενικές ερωτήσεις
2. **bookings@feelingshouses.gr** - Κρατήσεις

## Option 1: Hostinger Email (RECOMMENDED - Ήδη έχεις domain στο Hostinger)

### Steps:
1. **Login to Hostinger**: https://hpanel.hostinger.com
2. **Go to**: Emails → Create Email Account
3. **Create these accounts**:
   - Email: `info@feelingshouses.gr`
   - Password: [Choose strong password]
   - Storage: 10GB
   
   - Email: `bookings@feelingshouses.gr`
   - Password: [Choose strong password]
   - Storage: 10GB

4. **Access your emails**:
   - Webmail: https://webmail.hostinger.com
   - Or setup on phone/computer (IMAP/SMTP settings provided by Hostinger)

### Cost:
- **FREE** with Hostinger hosting plan
- Or €0.99/month per mailbox

---

## Option 2: Google Workspace (Professional)

### Steps:
1. Go to: https://workspace.google.com/
2. Choose "Business Starter" (€5.75/user/month)
3. Add domain: feelingshouses.gr
4. Verify domain (add TXT record to DNS)
5. Create email accounts

### Benefits:
- Gmail interface
- 30GB storage per user
- Google Drive, Calendar, Meet included
- Professional appearance

---

## Option 3: Email Forwarding (Quick & Free)

### Steps:
1. **Hostinger DNS Settings**:
   - Add email forwarding rules:
   - `info@feelingshouses.gr` → Forward to your personal Gmail
   - `bookings@feelingshouses.gr` → Forward to your personal Gmail

2. **Reply From**:
   - Setup Gmail to "Send mail as" info@feelingshouses.gr
   - SMTP settings from Hostinger

### Benefits:
- FREE
- Use your existing Gmail
- Can reply as info@feelingshouses.gr
